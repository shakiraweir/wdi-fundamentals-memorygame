Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Health Benefits

Not only are memory games fun, but they have great health benefits. Memory games have been shown to improve cognitive development, brain activity, working memory and reading performances. Prolonged use of memory games can help reduce dyslexia, slow signs of Alzheimer's and other cognitive impairments. So start exercising your brain and retain your brain fitness by playing Concentration today.
